# 30-Day Professional Roadmap

## Week 1
- Configure programs and scope sources.
- Establish baseline recon and asset inventory.
- Validate reproducibility of scripts.

## Week 2
- Add high-signal checks and templates.
- Build dedupe and prioritization habit.
- Improve evidence collection format.

## Week 3
- Increase manual validation depth on top programs.
- Optimize scan rate and reduce noise.
- Standardize report quality with peer checklist.

## Week 4
- Review acceptance and duplicate patterns.
- Focus on vulnerability classes with best payout-to-time ratio.
- Lock operational KPI dashboard.
